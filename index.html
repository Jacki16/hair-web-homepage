Welcome to my hair page

My name is Jacqueline Assiri and I specialize in various different braids and loc styles. I started getting into braiding at a very young age my mom owned a braiding salon, and even prior braided hair out of our home. Being around braiding I would often look at my aunties in the shop braid hair and always help them prep hair, or braid down the ends. Eventually, I started doing my sister and cousins hair to get some practice it wasn't the most neat at first but overtime I got really good. Freshman year of college I started doing a couple people hair just to really get some content and let people know I braided hair. I never did locs prior, but when of my friends had asked and I told him, I did...lol. Me being a fast learner I watched a tiktok video on how to start locs and they turned out great.

At Jacque's Cre8tions I service various clients to provide them with their hair needs. From loc styles, cornrows, braids and many more. Especially being on a college campus I'm working to provide afforable everyday looks for college students especially of color to feel comfortable and confident with their hair. Providing these services is a joy for me and my clients when they see the results of their hair. On my webpage you will be able to navigate and see some of the styles I've done on Instagram. And you'll also be able to book an appointment. It provides you with a break down of pricing as well to provide you with an estimate of how much the service will cost. You are able to pick a style of your choice ranging from braids where you can pick length, color, and size, to loc styles if you want a reguler retwist, two strand twist, barrels, or whatever style you may desire, and so much more. You will also be able to book dates and time slots through the site so we can be able to find a convient date that works for you and me to get your service provided.
